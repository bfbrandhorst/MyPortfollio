About Me

Hello, I'm Bethany! I am venturing out into this coding world coming from a background in veterinary medicine. Throughout the years I spent in that field I was a tried and true user of multiple interfaces. Those interfaces, whether they lie within actual diagnostic tools or the programs used by the staff, often left something to be desired. I frequently found myself wondering if the person who designed those interfaces knew what the actual users needed to do their job efficently. With my background and passion in medicine, by moving into coding and development my ultimate goal is to enhance the experience of users working in the medical field!
